### Recommended Outreach Method: Loom Video + Personalized Email

#### Step 1: Find Targets

- Use Google Maps to find businesses in Minneapolis
- Filter by: 3-4 star ratings (room to improve), <50 reviews, no online booking visible
- Use [GMB Everywhere](https://www.gmbeverywhere.com/) tool for quick audits

#### Step 2: Record 60-Second Loom Video

Show:
1. Their Google Business Profile
2. Call their number (show if it goes to voicemail)
3. Point out missing features (no booking, few reviews, slow response)
4. "I help businesses like yours fix this. Can we chat for 10 minutes?"

#### Step 3: Email Template

```
Subject: Quick video about [Business Name]'s Google listing

Hey [Name],

I was looking at local [industry] businesses in Minneapolis and noticed
a few things about [Business Name]'s online presence that might be
costing you leads.

I recorded a quick 60-second video walking through what I found:
[Loom Link]

Not trying to sell you anything - just thought you'd want to know.
Happy to chat if you want to fix any of it.

[Your Name]
```
